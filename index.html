02
First 2 Vans
Erika and Rok each order one van — 2 vehicles built simultaneously. This kicks off series production.

Tourne Mobil: buys Sprinter, installs floor heating & insulation (€60k/van).

Alfastreet: does everything else — furniture, equipment installation, finishing (€35k labor + all equipment).

Erika buys at production cost (~€140k instead of €225k retail).
03
Scale to 10/Year
From Q2 2027: series production at 10 vans/year, scaling to 15 by 2030.

Revenue split per vehicle:
€20k commission → whoever sells (Glam-van, Erika, or Alfastreet).
Remaining: 80% Glam-van / 20% Alfastreet.

Erika: 10% equity in Glam-van = dividends from Glam-van's 80% share. Plus €20k per customer she brings.
